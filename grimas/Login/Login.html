Login - login to grima

This grima checks your password, so you can use other grimas without further authentication. We can’t just allow every web-crawler to delete all our holdings.

This grima is called automatically if you try to access another grima while not logged in. After a successful login, you will be redirected to the grima you initially tried.

Input

  • login, password and instition as assigned by system administrator
